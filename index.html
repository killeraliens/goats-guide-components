The summit for all you extreme sound enthusiasts.

Goat's Guide is a networking tool and event bulletin without the bloated social feed. No suggested playlists, selfie albums, or "events near you" notifications. Our goal is to broaden the horizon for the heavy listeners, and empower the professional connections to make it happen.

Contribute to the database by creating a profile and posting events known to you.

Profile templates encourage skill sharing, past & current project mentions, availabilities, and points of contact. Event templates are designed to get the most important info up and on the map with minimal effort.
